§ 02 · About

A psychologist by training,
an analyst by practice.

I work at the intersection of consumer psychology and quantitative research, helping brands move past surface-level metrics to understand what their audiences are actually doing — and why. My foundation in psychology shapes how I design surveys; my experience as a research executive shapes how I read them.

At Jakpat I lead end-to-end research engagements for clients across FMCG, automotive, technology, and digital app sectors. The work spans brand health trackers, usage-and-attitude studies, name and concept testing, and bespoke market intelligence reports — often built on Indonesian-context data sources like GAIKINDO and primary regulatory texts.

Outside client work I publish ongoing market analysis on LinkedIn, with a current focus on Indonesia's automotive and EV transition.
